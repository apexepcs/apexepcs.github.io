About Us

APEX Engineering & Power Cooling Systems LLP is a trusted provider of comprehensive engineering and power cooling solutions for power plants and industrial facilities. We specialize in cooling tower inspection, NDCT and IDCT internals replacement, chemical cleaning and descaling, mechanical erection, shutdown maintenance, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

In addition to services, APEX undertakes industrial construction and engineering consultancy. The company also supplies industrial equipment, mechanical spares, hardware items, tools, and safety equipment (PPE). Driven by innovation, we design and develop advanced cooling tower components such as fills, drift eliminators, spray nozzles, and water distribution systems to enhance cooling efficiency and performance.
