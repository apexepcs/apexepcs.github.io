About APEX Engineering & Power Cooling Systems LLP

Trusted Engineering Partner for Industrial Cooling Solutions

APEX Engineering & Power Cooling Systems LLP is a trusted provider of cooling tower services, industrial engineering solutions, mechanical erection, shutdown maintenance, and industrial supply across India.


We specialize in cooling tower inspection, NDCT & IDCT internals replacement, chemical cleaning & descaling, Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), piping, fabrication, and industrial project support.


Our company also supplies cooling tower components such as fills, drift eliminators, spray nozzles, water distribution systems, mechanical spares, industrial hardware items, and safety equipment (PPE).
